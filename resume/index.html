Research

  • Dissertation

    In substantial, my researh work on IDPs(Intrinsically Disordered Proteins) can be described as a classical binary classification problem. In normal situation, the 3D structure of a protein is determined by its amino acid squence. We call the defined structure as orderd squence, meanwhile disordered squence which can not form determined 3D structure exist a big part in the protein world.

    In my work, I analyzed existed algorithms on prediction of disordered proteins. Of all the features used in those algorithms, PSSM and 10 physicochemical properties of amino acids I choose to compare existed machine learning algorithms and figure out whether RNN and CNN, the so-called Deep Learning methods, can be used effectively in this field.
